Sansera

Sansera was incorporated in 1981 in Bengaluru and commenced commercial production of passenger vehicle components in 1986. We grew our precision components manufacturing business organically in India by commencing supplies to the two-wheeler vertical in 1996, for off road vehicles in 2009, and to the light commercial vehicle vertical in 2011. We set up a manufacturing facility dedicated to high precision aluminium and titanium machined aerospace components in 2013. In April 2017, we acquired a 100% stake in Sansera Sweden, which has facilitated our entry into the heavy commercial vehicle vertical in the automotive sector, expanded our customer base and improved geographical access to OEMs outside India.

Branches

PowerTrain

Powertrain is like a car's heart and muscles combined. It's the part that makes a vehicle go! Think of it as the engine, transmission, and all the parts that transfer power to the wheels. In our student car club, Powertrain branch focuses on picking the right engine and making it work smoothly.
16 Jan,20

Vehicle Dynamics

Vehicle Dynamics is like the magic that makes a car dance on the road. It's all about how a vehicle moves, steers, and stays stable. In our student car project, Vehicle Dynamics branch is responsible for ensuring our car handles well, turns safely, and feels balanced while driving.
30 Nov,19

Structural Engineering

Structural Engineering is like the skeleton of a building or a bridge. It's about designing and building the strong bones that hold everything up. In our student car project, the Structural Engineering branch focuses on creating a sturdy chassis and body frame to support the vehicle's weight and keep it safe on the road.
6 Jul,19

Electrical Engineering

Electrical Engineering is like the nervous system of a car, handling all the electricity and electronics. It's about designing and installing the wiring, circuits, and components that power everything from lights to the engine. In our student car project, the Electrical Engineering branch ensures all electrical systems work smoothly, from headlights to stereo.
1 Jun,19

Aerodynamics

In racecar engineering, aerodynamics is pivotal for maximizing speed and handling. It involves shaping the car's body to minimize drag and generate downforce, improving traction and cornering capabilities. By optimizing airflow around the vehicle, engineers enhance performance, stability, and efficiency, crucial for success in competitive racing.
28 Feb,19

NON-Technical

A non-technical team consists of individuals with diverse backgrounds, skills, and expertise outside of STEM fields. They contribute to various aspects of a project or organization, such as marketing, sales, human resources, and administration, fostering creativity, collaboration, and holistic problem-solving to achieve common goals.
